Help

Number

You can think of the number as your money or point, that you can buy things with.


NPC

NPC is an acronym for Number Per Click and it shows how much your Number is going to increase after clicking.


NPS

NPS in an acronym for Number Per Second and it shows how much your Number is going to increase every second.


GEN

GEN means GENerator. It automaticly buys the NPC and NPS upgrades for you.


Infinity

After you reach Infinity(1.8*10^308), you get an Infinity. You can buy upgrades for these infinities in Infinitum.


Infinitum

You gain access to Infinitum, after you reach Infinity(1.8*10^308). In Infinitum you can buy upgrades for your infinities.
